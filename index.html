QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Mutoco ?

Mutoco Web3 Hub is focused on developing fully transparent, decentralized applications (DApps) on the Polygon Network, leveraging blockchain technology and smart contracts to create automated systems. Their solutions cater exclusively to DAO communities, offering NFT-gated access for secure and exclusive use. The hub’s primary goal is to provide partners with efficient, automated systems and decentralized DApps that enhance transparency and functionality within the blockchain ecosystem.

Why Mutoco is the best Choice for Communites ?

Mutoco Web3 Hub’s primary focus is to provide its partner communities with automated systems and fully decentralized DApps, ensuring seamless and efficient operations within the blockchain ecosystem.

What types of Communities can join in ?

Communities that can integrate with Mutoco include those operating within DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) frameworks, NFT-based projects, and any group looking for secure, transparent, and automated solutions on the blockchain. These communities typically seek to enhance their operations with decentralized applications (DApps) and smart contract-driven automation, ensuring better governance, security, and efficiency.

What types of DApps can Mutoco already provide to us ?

Mutoco is already developing DApps such as a Launchpad, NodePad, a Smartpool of Nodes based on tokenization (NFTs), Raffle Parties, and auctions for high-value NFTs, with more features on the way.
